# Module: orders, backlog, delivery, acceptance, and installed base

Use when revenue requires an order, manufacturing/delivery, installation, acceptance, or service over time.

## Recognition chain

```text
Demand/TAM
→ quote/design selection
→ order/bookings
→ backlog/RPO
→ manufacture
→ shipment
→ installation
→ acceptance/control transfer
→ recognized revenue
→ cash collection
```

Never collapse these states.

## Revenue components

- new systems/projects;
- contract service;
- spares/consumables;
- installed-base upgrades;
- mature/refurbished systems;
- deferred service.

## Backlog quality

Separate customer deposits, shipped-not-accepted systems, near-term orders, long-dated commitments, cancellable orders, and service obligations.

## Typical companies

Semiconductor equipment, power/cooling, data-center projects, industrial systems, and capacity-constrained suppliers.

## Failure modes

- bookings equal next-year revenue;
- support revenue treated as a fixed annuity;
- installation/acceptance ignored;
- customer fab or project readiness ignored;
- RPO timing and quality not decomposed.
