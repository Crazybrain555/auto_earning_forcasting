# Lens: enterprise recurring and hybrid software

This lens combines `module-recurring-contract-revenue.md`, `module-platform-usage-adoption.md` when usage is material, `module-orders-backlog-recognition.md` for hardware or acceptance, and `module-perimeter-and-accounting.md`. It applies to SaaS, term-license, maintenance, usage, IP and hybrid software companies.

## Required decomposition

- product family and revenue type;
- customer group, payer, user and procurement unit;
- recurring base, renewal, expansion/new-logo, usage and price;
- upfront IP/hardware/services and their recognition gates;
- deferred revenue, contract assets, RPO/backlog and billings;
- gross margin by revenue type;
- sales efficiency, implementation capacity and customer success constraints;
- SBC, commissions, capitalization/amortization, tax states and cash conversion.

## State and failure map

A signed deal is not recognized revenue. ARR is not revenue. Renewal rate without price, seat, product, cohort and usage context is not enough. A new accounting standard or migration must have a separate transition bridge. Discrete tax or accounting benefits are not operating leverage and do not increase FCF unless cash taxes actually fall.

## Readiness

Without direct cohort/renewal, contract-duration or recognition evidence, cap the revenue mechanism at `screen-grade` or `hypothesis-grade`. A consolidated forecast may be useful, but it must disclose the residual and show falsification triggers.
