# Module: contracts, RPO, joint ventures, and economic capital

Use when long-term agreements, customer advances, joint ventures, guarantees, or off-balance-sheet capital are material.

## Contract bridge

```text
Effective protected revenue
= Quantity commitment
× disclosed price-protection coverage
× execution rate
× recognition timing
```

Keep separate:

- RPO;
- contract liability/customer advance;
- quantity obligation;
- price floor/range;
- margin protection;
- execution/renegotiation;
- revenue recognition.

## Economic capital bridge

```text
Economic capital need
= consolidated capex
+ JV loans/equity contributions
+ equipment prepayments
+ guarantees/support payments
+ capital-like service commitments
- repayments/recoveries
```

Check for double counting against inventory, COGS, lease liabilities, and ordinary supplier payments.

## Typical companies

Memory/storage JVs, manufacturing alliances, long-term supply agreements, capacity reservations, and customer-funded infrastructure.
