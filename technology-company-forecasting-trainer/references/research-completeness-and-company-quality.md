# Research Readiness and Independent Judgment

Research is sufficient only when it supports a falsifiable causal estimate of
future revenue, operating profit and GAAP net income attributable for the
declared decision.  It is not sufficient because a package reaches a document,
word, source, lane, topic, company-quality-dimension or technology-row count.
Those counts may describe a package; they do not measure forecast ability.

This protocol separates what code can prove from what an independent analyst
must judge.

## Deterministic research contract

`scripts/validate_research_completeness.py` is a narrow integrity validator. In
strict mode it enforces only:

1. accepted evidence has point-in-time, recursively resolved root provenance;
2. every declared main-line carrier has a conclusion-critical measurement on
   that node or a declared causal upstream node;
3. each such measurement preserves construct, population, period, unit,
   vintage and method, and a claimed cross-check is genuinely independent and
   definition-compatible or has a recomputable numeric bridge;
4. Base assumptions that carry the main line cite eligible evidence, a
   re-executable perturbation and a declared falsification node;
5. a claim labeled `corroborated` actually contains an independent
   root/originating-team/measurement-method pair;
6. the independent research review is bound by hashes to the exact source
   manifest, graph, data-series register and material-assumption register;
7. the reviewer is not the builder, covers the frozen graph's principal
   contradiction and does not authorize readiness above its stated cap.

The validator reuses the shared provenance and data-series primitives. It does
not maintain a second definition of independence or comparability.

## What the validator does not judge

Code cannot decide whether:

- the selected industry boundary and profit pool are economically insightful;
- a source is noisy despite formally complete metadata;
- the chosen reference class and cycle regime are appropriate;
- competition, substitution or management response is modeled convincingly;
- a paper, patent or laboratory result transfers to manufacturing and customer
  adoption;
- an advantage is a durable moat or a temporary cycle benefit;
- another search is likely to change the forecast;
- the evidence as a whole warrants an active-investment conclusion.

These are independent judgments. A deterministic warning can point to a thin,
single-method or unresolved route, but it cannot manufacture an answer by
requiring more rows.

## Question-led materiality routing

At `evidence_system`, start from the causally sufficient thesis carriers, their serious
rival and the model's largest unknowns. For each material question state:

- the carrier, edge or accounting output it may change;
- the competing explanation;
- the discriminating observation and required measurement basis;
- the evidence permission sought;
- the forecast or posture consequence if the question remains unresolved;
- the stopping condition for further search.

Use `research_coverage_matrix.csv` as an open question register, not a fixed
topic checklist. Add industry structure, inventory/cycle, customer/supplier,
management, patents, papers, commercialization, regulation or capital
allocation only when the company's economics or uncertainty makes that route
material. A missing material route is a review finding; a non-material route
needs an economic reason, not a blank placeholder.

The historical financial base is still non-negotiable for a full-company
forecast, but it is established through comparable fact and statement
reconciliation—not by counting filing documents.

## Source depth and noise

Preserve original documents or anchored extracts whenever available. Record
measurement method, scope, vintage, revisions, conflicts, missingness and known
bias. Word count, document depth, source type and lane remain diagnostics for
the evidence reviewer. They never override construct mismatch or admit an
unsupported Base input.

One direct, well-defined measurement plus a genuinely independent comparable
cross-check can be more useful than twenty articles laundering the same press
release. Conversely, an audited filing can be authoritative yet too aggregated
or backward-looking for a specific forward driver.

## Independent research-quality review

Use `assets/templates/research_quality_review_template.json` after freezing the
research inputs and before the builder responds. The independent reviewer gets
the frozen source pack and executable graph, reconstructs a serious rival and
records its judgment without a document-count rubric.

The artifact contains:

- distinct builder and reviewer identities;
- hashes of the four frozen research inputs;
- the exact main-line carrier and falsification nodes;
- the rival hypothesis, evidence IDs, reasoning and judgment on the principal
  contradiction;
- an open-ended `material_judgments` list routed to issues that matter in this
  case;
- overall research sufficiency, readiness cap, rationale and unresolved
  material disagreements.

There is no minimum finding count and no mandatory list of sectors or topics.
When material, review questions ordinarily include data noise and conflicts,
industry boundary/profit pool, competition and cycle, commercialization or
paper-to-production transfer, normalized earning power and the evidence most
likely to falsify the thesis. The reviewer may add, combine or omit questions
based on the case, but must explain the economic consequence of each material
finding.

`adequate`, `limited` and `inadequate` are judgments, not scores. A `limited`
review preserves unresolved disagreements and caps readiness. An `inadequate`
review blocks publication of the affected conclusion. The builder may respond
afterward, but cannot edit the frozen review into agreement.

## Optional company-quality and technology routes

`company_quality_moat_register.csv` and
`technology_commercialization_register.csv` are materiality-routed schedules,
not universal row taxonomies.

When a moat claim is authored, its source/node/monitor/fade/ROIC links must
resolve and its evidence permission must be honest. When a technology route is
material, technical-evidence validation checks identifiers, conditions,
replication and quantified production bridges. Independent judgment still owns
whether the route is economically representative and whether competitors can
respond.

No blank table and no repeated fluent paragraph counts as analysis. No missing
optional table automatically proves a research failure either; the independent
review must decide whether the omitted mechanism could materially change
revenue, operating profit, attributable net income or value.

## Passing and readiness

A passing research validator means:

- the objective evidence-to-main-line contract is traceable;
- claims about independence and comparability are mechanically true;
- the independent judgment was made on the frozen case; and
- the delivery does not exceed that judgment's readiness cap.

It does **not** mean the system has proven predictive accuracy or that the
investment thesis is correct. Structural readiness and empirical predictive
performance are separate claims and require separate evidence.
