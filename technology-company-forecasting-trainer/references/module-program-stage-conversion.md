# Module: program and product stage conversion

Use when revenue depends on long design, qualification, or custom development programs.

## Stage chain

```text
Architecture discussion
→ funded NRE
→ tape-out / prototype
→ sample
→ qualification
→ production award
→ production ramp
→ material revenue
```

## Base permissions

- architecture/research: zero revenue;
- funded NRE: only disclosed NRE;
- tape-out/sample: option/tail only;
- qualification: limited probability-weighted ramp;
- production award: explicit ramp may enter Base;
- material revenue: full operating model.

## Required fields

Customer role, product type, generation, NRE, tape-out, qualification, award, expected ramp, content, ASP, share, second source, capacity, cancellation risk, and recognition unit.

## Typical companies

Custom ASIC, optical interconnect, networking, auto semiconductors, industrial design wins, and emerging products.

## Failure modes

- design win equals revenue;
- future customer or award backfilled into historical Base;
- complete module/system BOM applied to a component supplier;
- ramp timing and second-source risk omitted.
